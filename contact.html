Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a tour?

You can book directly through our website, call us, or visit our office. Simply choose your package, fill out the booking form, and our team will contact you to confirm details and process payment.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30+ days before departure receive a full refund minus processing fees. 15-29 days: 50% refund. Less than 15 days: no refund unless covered by travel insurance.

Do you offer travel insurance?

Yes! We highly recommend travel insurance and can help you choose the right coverage for your trip, including medical, cancellation, and baggage protection.

Can I customize a tour package?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating personalized itineraries. Contact us with your preferences, budget, and interests, and we'll design a custom package just for you.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), bank transfers, and PayPal. Payment plans are available for packages over $3,000.
